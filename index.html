About Us

Incorporated in Kenya in 2010, Magari Africa is a leasing and rental of passenger motor vehicle of all classes inclusive of executive chauffer services. We are based in Nairobi at Jabavu Lane off Arwings Kodhek road; this gives us proximity to the major business areas and the central business district, giving us an opportunity to serve our diverse range of clients with utmost ease. Magari Africa is made of a team of professionals with experience in fleet management and a passion to deliver unparalleled services. With combined experience of over ten years in the business you can rest assured that your travel needs are in good hands. At Magari Africa, every effort is made by our personnel to satisfy and even supersede our client’s expectations and with several value added services like infant car seats, navigation maps, multi-lingual chauffeurs, etc included at a marginal cost you can’t go wrong with us. The result of this concerted effort to satisfy our clients has resulted in Magari Africa receiving several repeat clients. Your safety and comfort is at all times our concern and we keep our cars in the excellent condition and our drivers in the best physical condition to serve you. We take pride in our corporate commitment to excellence following strict ethical standards, while maintaining honesty and moral soundness. We reckon that our area of business calls for utmost sincerity from the onset. This ensures a happy and satisfied clientele at the end of every lease or rental.
